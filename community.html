Community Support

Peer Consulting believes in giving back to the community. Hence, we are dedicated to supporting the communities that we live and operate in.

  • We provide internship opportunities to students and recent graduates
  • We support 501(C)(3) and FCRA organizations
  • We volunteer to strengthen our communities

Community Service

We serve our communities with empathy and compassion.

Summer Youth Employment Program (SYEP)

We collaborate with the New York City Department of Youth and Community Development (DYCD) and offer employment to youth between the ages of 14 and 24 with career exploration opportunities and paid work experience each summer.

Aarati for Girls

Aarti for Girls fosters and supports abandoned girls in Kadapa, India by providing them with a home and opportunity. With over 6,000 children passing through its doors, Aarti Home has become a haven for children who have suffered abuse, trafficking, and abandonment.

Sankara Nethralaya

Sankara Nethralaya provides ophthalmic care - surgery and outpatient treatments for free to the economically underprivileged.

Akshaya Patra

Akshaya Patra Foundation is a secular not-for-profit organization that cooks, transports, and serves 1.8 million midday meals to government school children in 19,257 schools across 14 States and 2 Union Territories in India, every day to address classroom hunger.
